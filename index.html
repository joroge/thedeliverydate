💪 Physiological Tips

🌱 First Trimester Tips
  • 💊Take prenatal vitamins with folic acid daily
  • 💧Stay hydrated - drink 8-10 glasses of water daily
  • 😴Get adequate rest - aim for 7-9 hours of sleep
  • 🚶‍♀️Practice gentle exercise like walking or swimming
  • 🚫Avoid alcohol, smoking, and high-mercury fish
  • 🍽️Manage morning sickness with small, frequent meals
  • 👩‍⚕️Schedule your first prenatal appointment
  • 🧘‍♀️Take deep breaths to manage anxiety
  • 👙Wear comfortable, supportive bras
  • 🧴Start using pregnancy-safe skincare products
ACOG CDC WHO
🌸 Second Trimester Tips
  • 💊Continue prenatal vitamins and add calcium if needed
  • 🤸‍♀️Practice kegel exercises to strengthen pelvic floor
  • 😴Sleep on your side, preferably left side
  • ⚖️Monitor weight gain - aim for 1-2 pounds per week
  • 🧘‍♀️Stay active with prenatal yoga or swimming
  • 📋Start planning your birth preferences
  • 🎓Consider prenatal classes
  • 🩺Monitor for signs of gestational diabetes
  • 🛏️Use pregnancy pillows for better sleep
  • 👶Begin talking to your baby
Mayo Clinic BabyCenter March of Dimes
🌺 Third Trimester Tips
  • 🎒Pack your hospital bag by 36 weeks
  • 🫁Practice breathing exercises for labor
  • 👶Monitor baby's movements daily
  • 🛏️Sleep with extra pillows for comfort
  • 💧Stay hydrated to prevent swelling
  • 🤱Prepare for breastfeeding
  • ⚠️Watch for signs of preterm labor
  • 🔬Get Group B Strep test around 36 weeks
  • 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦Prepare siblings for new baby arrival
  • 😴Rest as much as possible before delivery
